Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

GauchoTech ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered phone ordering services for restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through our phone ordering system:

  • Customer order information: Name, phone number, delivery address, and order details provided by callers when placing an order.
  • Restaurant partner information: Business name, owner name, phone number, and email address provided during onboarding.
  • Call data: Call recordings and transcriptions for quality assurance and order accuracy.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and transmit food orders to restaurant partners.
  • Send SMS order notifications to restaurant owners or designated staff.
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our ordering system.
  • Communicate with restaurant partners about their account and service.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Restaurant partners: Customer order details are shared with the restaurant fulfilling the order.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services (such as Vapi for AI voice processing and call management, Twilio for SMS delivery and telephony, and Deepgram for speech recognition) that process data on our behalf to operate our service.

4. SMS Messaging

Restaurant partners consent to receive SMS order notifications when they sign up for our service. These messages contain order details for fulfillment purposes only. Restaurant partners may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message or contacting us directly.

5. Data Security

We rely on the security measures provided by our third-party service providers, including Vapi, Twilio, and Deepgram, to protect the data processed through our system. We limit access to customer information to authorized personnel only. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain order data and call recordings for as long as necessary to provide and improve our services. Restaurant partners may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify restaurant partners of any material changes via email or SMS.
